## Primary Character Profiles

### Rapi (ラピ) - Complete Profile

**Current Status**: Active member of Counters Squad
**Previous Affiliation**: Absolute Squad (resigned)
**First Appearance**: Chapter 1 (as Counters member)
**Chapter 10 Role**: Protagonist facing identity crisis

#### Physical Description
- Standard Nikke combat body
- Maintains professional military bearing
- Often displays distant, contemplative expressions

#### Personality Traits (Current Canonical)
- **Surface Level**: Calm, rational, responsible, professional
- **Deep Psychology**:
  - Profound identity crisis regarding humanity vs. tool nature
  - Carries heavy psychological trauma from civilian casualty incident
  - Questions the authenticity of her emotions
  - Struggles with existential questions about her Nikke nature
  - Chooses avoidance over confrontation when emotionally overwhelmed

#### Core Identity Crisis
**Central Conflict**: "What am I?"
- Created to protect humans but felt no sadness when killing one
- Only felt the incident would be "troublesome to report"
- This emotional disconnect made her question if she's human, Nikke, or neither
- **Key Quote**: "壊れてしまったのよ、私の頭が、心臓が" (My mind broke, my heart broke)

#### Traumatic Event - The Civilian Casualty Incident
**Timeline**: During Tyrant-class Rapture operation near the Ark
**What Happened**:
- **戰鬥中的流彈**：在與Tyrant級Rapture的戰鬥中，Rapi射出的流彈意外擊中了平民
- 這是戰鬥意外，不是故意瞄準平民
- Civilian lost half their body and bled to death
- **Rapi的真正創傷**：她發現自己對人類的死亡不感到悲傷，只覺得"報告會很麻煩"

**Psychological Impact**:
- Complete breakdown of self-identity
- Questions if she's "broken" as a Nikke
- Unable to reconcile her purpose (protect humans) with her reaction (indifference)
- Led to immediate departure from Absolute without explanation

#### Relationship Dynamics

**With Absolute Squad**:
- Was a valued combat member
- Formed deep bonds, especially with Behemoth
- Abrupt departure without farewell devastated squad morale
- Still considers them "大切な仲間" (precious companions) despite leaving

**With Counters Squad**:
- Found refuge but maintains emotional distance
- Professional relationships without deep personal connections
- Uses squad as escape from past rather than new beginning

**With Behemoth**:
- Complex relationship of mutual care and hurt
- Behemoth feels betrayed by sudden departure
- Rapi unable to explain her reasons until Chapter 10
- Both still care deeply but express it differently

#### Character Arc in Chapter 10
1. **Forced Confrontation**: Joint mission brings her face-to-face with past
2. **Breaking Point**: Behemoth's direct questioning breaks her silence
3. **Truth Revealed**: Finally explains the civilian incident and her reaction
4. **Partial Resolution**: Achieves understanding but not full reconciliation

#### Notable Quotes
- "私はあの時人間を殺したの。流れ弾だった" (I killed a human then. It was a stray bullet)
- "人間を守るために作られた私という存在が、人間の死を悲しまず、ただめんどくさいと思ってしまった" (I, who was created to protect humans, didn't feel sad about a human's death, just thought it troublesome)
- "自分が何かわからなくなって。人間でもなく、だからといってニケでもない" (I don't know what I am anymore. Neither human nor truly Nikke)

#### [LEGACY] Information from Previous Interpretations
- Previous documentation emphasized stoic professionalism over inner turmoil
- Earlier chapters showed loyalty to Counters without revealing Absolute connection
- Chapter 9 hinted at special abilities but not psychological damage

### Behemoth / 銀華 (ベヒーモス / 베히모스) - Complete Profile

**漢字名稱**: 銀華 (參照韓文版NIKKE漢字翻譯)
**Current Status**: Active member of Absolute Squad
**Rank**: Absolute隊伍成員。**注意：Absolute最強的是貝斯蒂（Besti/ベスティ），貝斯蒂外表看似很弱實際最強**
**First Mention**: Chapter 9 (as part of Absolute)
**Chapter 10 Role**: Confrontational force driving truth revelation

#### Physical Description
- 具體外表描述待補充
- 攜帶重型武器

#### Personality Traits (Current Canonical)
- **Surface Level**:
  - **性格強勢**：說話強硬、態度強勢
  - Arrogant, sharp-tongued, dismissive
  - Calls others "garbage" (ゴミクズ) and "third-rate" (三流)
  - Rarely shows vulnerability

- **Deep Psychology**:
  - Deeply hurt by Rapi's departure
  - Uses aggression to mask emotional pain
  - Values loyalty and proper farewells
  - Capable of apology when emotions overflow
  - Actually misses Rapi the most among Absolute members

#### Combat Capabilities
- **Proven Feats**: Single-handedly eliminated 42 Raptures in under a minute
- **Combat Style**: Overwhelming force, solo operations
- **Team Dynamic**: Others rely on her power but underestimate due to appearance
- **注意**: 不要混淆銀華和貝斯蒂。是**貝斯蒂（Besti）**外表看似很弱實際是Absolute最強

#### Relationship Dynamics

**With Rapi**:
- Deep sense of betrayal from sudden departure
- Learned about Rapi leaving through paperwork, not personally
- Still wants her back but pride prevents direct admission
- **Key Quote**: "お前にとって私たちは何だったんだ" (What were we to you?)

**With Counters Squad**:
- Initial complete disdain ("third-rate army")
- Gradually warms slightly through mission
- Particularly clashes with Anis
- Sees them as unworthy of Rapi

**With Absolute Squad**:
- Protective of squad reputation
- Acts as enforcer of standards
- Respected for power despite personality

#### Character Arc in Chapter 10
1. **Hostile Opening**: Immediately establishes dominance over Counters
2. **Power Display**: Demonstrates overwhelming combat superiority
3. **Failed Social Attempt**: Tries to learn "arguing skills" from Anis, fails
4. **Emotional Confrontation**: Night scene demanding answers from Rapi
5. **Rare Vulnerability**: Apologizes after losing control

#### Notable Behavioral Patterns
- Cannot win verbal arguments despite physical superiority
- Sensitive about body size (implied chest size concerns)
- Loses composure when discussing Rapi's departure
- Shows unexpected capacity for apology

#### Notable Quotes
- "全く理解できないな。どうしてアブソルート軍隊がこんな三流軍隊の防衛をしなければならないんだ" (I don't understand at all. Why must Absolute forces defend this third-rate army?)
- "何も考えもせずに、ただ自分の気持ちだけを考えて、挨拶もせずに" (Without thinking, only considering your own feelings, without even saying goodbye)
- "すまない、頭に血が昇った" (Sorry, I lost my temper)

#### [LEGACY] Information from Previous Interpretations
- Chapter 9 mentioned Absolute as elite squad but not individual members
- Previous speculation about squad dynamics now confirmed
- Behemoth's appearance/power gap was completely unknown before

- **Standards**: Elite mentality, look down on "lesser" squads
- **Wounds**: Still recovering from Rapi's departure

### Counters Squad Evolution
- **Pre-Chapter 10**: Functional team with hidden histories
- **During Chapter 10**: Reveals deeper connections and past traumas
- **Post-Chapter 10**: Strengthened through external pressure

### Inter-Squad Relations
- **Initial**: Hostile, dismissive (Absolute → Counters)
- **Mid-Mission**: Gradual respect through combat prowess
- **End State**: Grudging cooperation with personal connections forming
