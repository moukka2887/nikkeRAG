## Basic Information

- **Full Name**: Crown
- **Japanese**: クラウン
- **English**: Crown
- **Chinese**: 皇冠
- **Squad**: Unlimited (with Chime)
- **Manufacturer**: Tetra Line
- **Role**: Self-proclaimed ruler, Kingdom builder
- **Special Title**: "Queen" of Crown Kingdom (クラウン王国)

## Personality Traits

### Core Characteristics
- **Regal Bearing**: Speaks and acts with royal authority
- **Ambitious**: Desires to establish her own kingdom on the surface
- **Protective**: Of her "subjects" and kingdom
- **Strategic**: Tactical thinker in combat situations
- **Partnership-focused**: Works closely with Chime

### Speech Pattern
- Uses royal/formal language ("我" pronouns in some versions)
- References kingdom and subjects frequently
- Commands rather than requests
- Maintains dignified tone even in crisis

## Background

### Origins
- Part of Tetra Line's mass production line
- Formed Unlimited squad with Chime
- Developed delusions of grandeur or genuine royal aspirations
- Seeks to establish "Crown Kingdom" on the surface

### Crown Kingdom Concept
- Vision of establishing a kingdom in reclaimed surface territory
- Collects "treasures" and "subjects" for future kingdom
- Partner Chime serves as primary supporter/advisor
- Kingdom exists more as aspiration than reality currently

## Abilities & Combat Style

### Combat Role
- **Type**: Supporter/Defender hybrid
- **Specialty**: Area control and protection
- **Weapon Class**: [Information from game stats]

### Tactical Approach
- Prioritizes protecting "subjects" (allies)
- Maintains defensive positions
- Coordinates with Chime for combination attacks
- Strategic rather than aggressive combat style

## Relationships

### With Chime
- **Status**: Partner and fellow kingdom builder
- **Dynamic**: Mutual support and shared delusions/dreams
- **Trust Level**: Absolute
- Crown and Chime operate as inseparable unit

### With Other Nikkes
- Treats other Nikkes as potential "subjects"
- Offers protection in exchange for loyalty
- Can be demanding but ultimately protective
- Respected for combat abilities despite eccentricities

### With Humans
- Views Commander as important ally but not superior
- Maintains independence while cooperating
- Offers kingdom citizenship to favored humans
- Complex relationship with authority structures

## Psychological Analysis

### Identity Construction
Crown has constructed elaborate royal identity:
- Could be coping mechanism for war trauma
- Might represent desire for control in chaotic world
- Partnership with Chime reinforces shared narrative
- "Kingdom" represents hope for better future

### Leadership Psychology
Despite fantastical framing, shows real leadership:
- Protective instincts toward others
- Strategic thinking capability
- Ability to inspire through vision
- Maintains morale through role-playing

## Trivia
