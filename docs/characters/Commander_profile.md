## Position and Authority

### Official Status
- Tactical commander assigned to Counters squad (primary)
- Commands other squads as missions require
- Reports to Anderson (Vice Commander)
- Subject to Central Government oversight
- Limited autonomy despite command authority

### Command Limitations
- **Resources**: Restricted by Central Government
- **Tactical freedom**: Often overruled by bureaucracy
- **NIKKE assignment**: Cannot freely choose squad members
- **Mission selection**: Assigned rather than chosen
- **Political power**: Minimal despite tactical success

### Unique Circumstances
- Exiled to "Outpost" (前哨基地) after Ch2 incident
- Loss of 2-month resources from destroyed power plant
- Operates from remote location (less oversight = more freedom)
- Protected by influential figures (Ingrid, Anderson, Scarlet)

## Personality Traits (Player-Variable)

### Core Consistent Traits
Regardless of player choices, Commander demonstrates:
- **Tactical flexibility**: Unconventional strategies (Ch2 sewer infiltration vs standard assault)
- **NIKKE respect**: Treats NIKKE as people, not disposable tools
- **Protective instinct**: Prioritizes squad safety over mission objectives
- **Moral integrity**: Questions unethical orders (Ch14 Maria pursuit)
- **Strategic innovation**: Finds creative solutions others miss
- **Calm under pressure**: Maintains composure during crises

### Observed Behavioral Patterns
- **Decision-making**: Analyzes situations before committing
- **Leadership style**: Collaborative rather than authoritarian
- **Risk tolerance**: Willing to take calculated risks to protect subordinates
- **Ethical framework**: Values individual lives over resource optimization
- **Communication**: Direct and honest with squad members

## Tactical Capabilities

### Strategic Strengths
- **Unconventional thinking**: Ch2 sewer infiltration vs frontal assault
- **Resource optimization**: Works within severe constraints
- **Adaptive planning**: Adjusts tactics mid-operation
- **Squad psychology**: Understands individual NIKKE motivations and limitations
- **Risk assessment**: Balances objectives vs casualties

### Notable Tactical Decisions

**Ch2 - Sewer Infiltration**:
- Chose covert approach over standard frontal assault
- Anis noted this was unusual - most commanders default to firepower
- Demonstrated tactical creativity

**Ch2 - Power Plant Destruction**:
- Chose to destroy facility to enable squad retreat
- Sacrificed resources to save NIKKE lives
- Led to exile but preserved squad

**Ch14 - Maria Protection**:
- Defied Central Government pursuit orders
- Protected Maria despite political pressure
- Coordinated multi-faction defense (Counters + Scarlet + Anderson)
- Chose to send Maria to surface for safety

### Command Philosophy
Based on observed patterns:
1. **NIKKE are people**: Not disposable resources
2. **Squad cohesion > bureaucracy**: Team bonds matter more than politics
3. **Creative solutions**: Question conventional tactics
4. **Calculated risk**: Accept consequences for ethical choices
5. **Long-term thinking**: Build trust over short-term gains
