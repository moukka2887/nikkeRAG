## Core World Systems

### The Nikke Emotional System - Expanded Understanding

**New Canonical Information (Chapter 10)**

#### Emotional Programming vs. Genuine Feeling
- **The Central Paradox**: Nikkes are programmed to protect humans but can experience emotional "malfunctions"
- **Rapi's Case Study**:
  - Expected reaction to killing human: Profound grief, guilt, system shutdown
  - Actual reaction: Annoyance about paperwork
  - System interpretation: "壊れてしまった" (I broke)

#### Implications for Nikke Consciousness
1. **Emotions May Not Be Fully Programmed**: The ability to "break" suggests genuine emotional capacity
2. **Self-Awareness of Dysfunction**: Nikkes can recognize when their responses don't match programming
3. **Identity Crisis Capability**: Can experience existential questions about their nature
4. **No Repair Protocol**: No mention of system restoration or debugging for emotional anomalies

#### System Design Philosophy
- Created with expectation of specific emotional responses
- No apparent safeguards for emotional deviation
- Nikkes expected to self-regulate or remove themselves if "broken"
- Design assumes emotional responses align with protective mission

**[LEGACY] Previous Understanding**
- Earlier chapters suggested Nikke emotions were fully genuine
- No prior indication of programmable emotional responses
- Identity questions focused on human vs. machine, not programming integrity

### Memory Erasure Technology (記憶消去)

**Confirmed Mechanics (Chapter 10)**

#### Standard Operation
- Routinely applied to Nikkes for unknown reasons
- Can target specific memories or time periods
- Considered standard procedure by command structure
- Not universally effective (Commander immune)

#### The Commander's Immunity
- **Confirmed Unique**: Emma's test proves complete immunity
- **Verification Method**: Remembers embarrassing/private first encounters
- **Significance**: Makes Commander invaluable as permanent witness
- **Trust Implications**: Nikkes can form lasting bonds knowing memories persist

#### Social Impact
- Memory erasure creates isolation between Nikkes and handlers
- Commander's immunity allows for genuine long-term relationships
- Nikkes value being remembered as proof of existence
- Creates power imbalance (most humans can make Nikkes forget)

**[LEGACY] Previous Understanding**
- Chapter 9 hinted at memory manipulation
- Full scope and Commander's immunity now confirmed
- Social implications not previously explored

### Military Squad Structure

**Absolute Squad - Elite Force Profile**

#### Organizational Position
- Designation: Elysion Corporation's premier combat unit
- Reputation: Considered superior to standard squads
- Authority: Can refuse missions, requires CEO approval for deployment
- Equipment: Latest Elysion technology, custom configurations

#### Combat Doctrine
- **Individual Excellence**: Members can solo large engagements
- **Minimal Coordination**: Trust in individual capability over tactics
- **Overwhelming Force**: Preference for decisive power over strategy
- **No Weak Links**: Every member combat-capable at elite level

#### Internal Culture
- Strong bonds between members
- Expectation of loyalty and proper farewells
- Shared trauma from Rapi's departure
- Pride in superiority over "lesser" squads

#### Power Scaling
- Behemoth: Can eliminate 42 Raptures in <60 seconds solo
- Standard squad: Would require full team for same threat
- Implication: One Absolute member equals entire regular squad

**Counters Squad - Irregular Unit**

#### Organizational Position
- Designation: Special operations unit, unconventional composition
- Reputation: Seen as "third-rate" by elite units
- Reality: More capable than appearance suggests
- Authority: Limited, relies on special permissions

#### Unique Aspects
- Houses former Absolute member (Rapi)
- Rapi has memory erasure immunity (from Unchained exposure)
- Overcomes resource limitations through teamwork
- Building reputation through results not pedigree

**[LEGACY] Squad Understanding**
- Previous chapters didn't establish clear squad hierarchies
- Absolute's existence mentioned in Chapter 9 but not detailed
- Power scaling now definitively established

### Nikke Existence Philosophy

**The Purpose Paradox**

#### Design Intent
- Created specifically to protect humanity
- Emotional responses assumed to align with purpose
- Failure to feel "appropriate" emotions seen as malfunction

#### Reality
- Emotional responses can deviate from intent
- No repair mechanism for emotional "errors"
- Nikkes must reconcile purpose with experience
- Identity crisis when purpose and feeling conflict

**The Memory Value System**

#### For Nikkes
- Being remembered validates existence
- Memory erasure threatens identity continuity
- Permanent witness (Commander) provides stability
- Shared memories create genuine bonds

#### For Command Structure
- Memory erasure maintains control
- Selective memory preserves operational security
- Commander's immunity creates unique dynamic
- Information asymmetry as power tool
