# Core Concept: Sensation Manipulation Technology

## Discovered Abilities

### Type 1: Sensation Nullification (Yuni)
- **Effect**: Complete removal of specific sensory input
- **Delivery**: Projected energy ("shooting" motion)
- **Duration**: Unknown, appears temporary
- **Selectivity**: Can target specific sensations

### Type 2: Sensation Exchange (Mihara)
- **Effect**: Bidirectional sensory pathway between two entities
- **Complexity**: Higher than nullification
- **Control**: User maintains pathway control
- **Experience**: Full sensory overlap possible

## Universal Effectiveness Phenomenon

### The Fundamental Question
*"ラプチャー向けに開発された能力がなぜニケや人間にまで使える？"*
(Why do abilities developed for Raptures work on Nikkes and humans too?)

### Hypothesis 1: Shared Consciousness Architecture
All three entity types (Human, Nikke, Rapture) share fundamental consciousness properties:
- Basic sensory processing systems
- Neural-equivalent pathways
- Consciousness quantum states

**Supporting Evidence**:
- Abilities work identically on all three
- No mentioned calibration needed between targets
- Immediate effectiveness

**Implications**:
- Raptures have consciousness comparable to humans
- Nikkes retain full human consciousness architecture
- Fundamental unity of awareness across species

### Hypothesis 2: Transcendent Technology
Missilis developed technology that operates above normal consciousness categories:
- Quantum consciousness manipulation
- Direct reality-perception interface
- Sub-quantum information transfer

**Supporting Evidence**:
- Even creators don't understand mechanism
- No existing technology can replicate
- Breaks established Nikke limitations

**Implications**:
- Technology beyond current understanding
- Possible non-human technology origin
- Syuen has access to unique resources

### Hypothesis 3: Rapture-Derived Enhancement
The abilities are based on reverse-engineered Rapture technology:
- Raptures have advanced consciousness manipulation
- Humans adapted this for their use
- Creates bridge between all consciousness types

**Supporting Evidence**:
- Abilities primarily designed for Rapture capture
- Raptures showing intelligent behavior
- Technology seems alien to human understanding

**Implications**:
- Raptures more advanced than believed
- Humans using enemy technology
- Dangerous precedent for human enhancement

# Conclusion

Chapter 03 fundamentally reshapes our understanding of consciousness in the NIKKE universe. The introduction of sensation manipulation abilities that work universally across humans, Nikkes, and Raptures suggests either:

1. **All consciousness shares fundamental architecture** - Revolutionary if true
2. **Technology exists that transcends categories** - Dangerous implications
3. **Raptures are more human-like than known** - Changes entire war context

The existence of Yuni and Mihara proves that Nikke limitations are artificial and breakable. If Syuen can create Nikkes that bypass core restrictions, what other rules can be broken? If Raptures have sensations and possibly consciousness, what does that mean for the war?

These revelations suggest the NIKKE universe operates on deeper principles than previously understood, with consciousness itself being a manipulatable, transferable, and shareable phenomenon rather than fixed individual property.
